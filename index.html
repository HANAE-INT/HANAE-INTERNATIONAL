About HANAE International

Founded in 2022 by Ananya Sharma, HANAE International is a vertically integrated Korean skincare manufacturer dedicated to delivering science-backed formulations at scale. We import from our Korean plant and manage global exports to Australia, New Zealand and Europe. We serve large retailers, distribution partners and brands seeking reliable manufacturing partners.

Our Philosophy

We combine the purity of natural actives with advanced Korean R&D to create efficacious skincare that performs. Our approach emphasises safety, sustainability and measurable results.

R&D & Formulation

Our formulations are developed with established Korean labs and dermatologists to meet diverse market needs. From gentle hydrating essences to peptide-rich serums, every formula undergoes rigorous testing.
