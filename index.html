About HANAE International

Founded in 2022 by Ananya Sharma, HANAE International is a vertically integrated Korean skincare manufacturer dedicated to delivering science-backed formulations at scale. We import from our Korean plant and manage global exports to Australia, New Zealand and Europe. We serve large retailers, distribution partners and brands seeking reliable manufacturing partners.

Our Philosophy & Approach

We combine the purity of natural actives with advanced Korean R&D to create efficacious skincare that performs. Our approach emphasises safety, sustainability and measurable results in every product batch.

R&D & Formulation Partnership

Our formulations are developed with established Korean labs and dermatologists to meet diverse market needs. From gentle hydrating essences to peptide-rich serums, every formula undergoes rigorous testing and stability checks before scaling.

Wholesale, B2B, and Scale

HANAE operates on wholesale terms only ‚Äî tailored Minimum Order Quantities (MOQs), bulk cartons, and long-term supply agreements. We do not process single-piece retail orders; our systems are optimised for volume and consistency, ensuring the best value for our partners.

Korean Manufacturing Facility

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing plant in South Korea uses automated lines and robust Quality Assurance (QA) processes designed for international export. We maintain strict GMP practices, in-house stability testing and offer sustainable packaging options.

03. Packaging & Customization
